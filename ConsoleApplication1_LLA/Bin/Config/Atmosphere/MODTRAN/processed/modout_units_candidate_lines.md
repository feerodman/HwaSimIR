## Conversion Required Before SI Relabeling

```text
L_lambda[W/(m^2 sr um)] = L_nu[W/(cm^2 sr cm^-1)] * 1e8 / lambda_um^2
E_lambda[W/(m^2 um)]    = E_nu[W/(cm^2 cm^-1)]    * 1e8 / lambda_um^2
```
