Reusable patterns:

- Chinese business copy should use Microsoft YaHei/微软雅黑-style sans serif.
- Primary blue follows template dark blue around #124B98, accent blue #4976D1, secondary green #4CB494, risk red #C43D32, neutral gray #E9EEF7.
- Use compact executive text, not paragraph-heavy proposal prose.

Insertion contract:
- Because the source deck mostly contains empty placeholders and the new deck needs product, architecture, roadmap, and financial proof objects, the final build uses rendered source-slide backgrounds as the template chrome and overlays editable artifact-tool text/shapes.
